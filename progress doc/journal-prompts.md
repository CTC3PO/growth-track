## Source 1: Camille Styles (80 prompts)
*Source: https://camillestyles.com/wellness/health/mental-health/daily-journal-prompts/*

### Gratitude Prompts
1. What are three great things that happened yesterday?
2. What are 10 things that bring you joy?
3. What are you looking forward to right now? If you can't think of anything, what can you do to change that?
4. What is one totally-free thing that's transformed your life?
5. What things in your life would you describe as priceless?
6. What are 10 things you're actively enjoying about life right now?
7. Write about the most fun you had recently. What were you doing and who were you with?
8. Write about an act of kindness that someone did for you that took you by surprise.
9. What are some of your favorite ways to show the people in your life that you love them?
10. Reflect on a moment of profound beauty that you recently experienced. What about it surprised you and drew you in?
11. In this moment, what are three things in your life that you feel the most grateful for?
12. Write five guilty pleasures you don't feel guilty about.
13. In what ways have you felt supported by friends, family, or your community recently?
14. Name three healthy habits you started within the last year that have changed your life for the better.
15. Describe your space. What do you love about it?
16. What are your favorite things to eat?
17. What are three small, seemingly insignificant moments from the past week that brought you joy?
18. Write about someone who inspires you. What qualities do they have that you admire?
19. What are five things your younger self would be amazed by or proud of in your life now?
20. Describe a recent challenge you overcame. What did it teach you, and how are you stronger because of it?

### Reflective Prompts
1. Name the top three emotions you are feeling at the moment. What are the emotions you want to feel today?
2. What is the one thing you would tell your teenage self if you could?
3. What is your body craving at the moment?
4. What are 10 questions you wish you had the answers to right now?
5. What do you know to be true today that you didn't know a year ago?
6. What are you scared of right now?
7. What's not working in your life right now?
8. Write about someone you miss. What do you miss about them? How do they make you feel?
9. Picture someone who you've experienced a conflict with in the past and try to drop into their perspective. What were they feeling at the time? If it's available to you, how can you express sympathy for their experience?
10. What areas of your life are causing you stress? What areas of your life are bringing you joy?
11. What would you describe as being the greatest accomplishment of your life so far?
12. If someone was to describe your life story back to you, which three events would you want them to highlight the most?
13. What has been the most transformative year in your life so far?
14. What is your earliest childhood memory?
15. How has your relationship to self-love grown and strengthened over the past five years?
16. What have you learned to forgive yourself for?
17. What does your ideal day look like, from start to finish? What steps can you take today to make it feel more like that?
18. When was the last time you felt truly at peace with yourself? What made that moment possible?
19. What is one fear you've faced in the past that you are proud of overcoming?
20. If you could change one thing about the way you show up in the world, what would it be, and why?

### Goal-Setting Prompts
1. Describe your perfect home. Where is it, what does it look like, and who do you share it with?
2. When you were younger, what did you want to be when you grew up and why?
3. If failure wasn't possible, what would you be doing right now?
4. If you only had one year left of life, what would you do?
5. In another life, who would you want to be? Write out this character, what they do for a living, their personality traits, etc.
6. Reflect on your career and personal goals. Are there parallels between the two? How are they the same?
7. If you could master one skill, what would it be?
8. What are new ways you can measure progress this year?
9. What is standing in your way of reaching your goals?
10. Who are the people you trust the most to help you create the life you've always dreamed of?
11. What habits and actions can you incorporate into your daily routine to help you prioritize your time?
12. What would it feel like to step out of your comfort zone? How can you step out of it more this year?
13. What talents or skills do you want to build and strengthen?
14. What challenges have you overcome in the past? How has doing so made your life more vibrant?
15. What's a commitment you can make to yourself every day to grow more this year?
16. What is the one thing you've always wanted to achieve but haven't yet? What steps can you take today?
17. What does success look like to you right now? How has your definition of success evolved over the years?
18. What are three specific goals you'd like to accomplish this year, and how can you break them down into manageable steps?
19. What would you do if you felt completely fearless? How can you embrace that boldness in your current life?
20. What's one big dream you've been putting off? What's the first step you can take today to move closer?

### Values-Based Prompts
1. What top three qualities do you value most in life?
2. In what ways are you acting outside of those values?
3. In what ways are you acting in alignment with them?
4. What do you want to invite more of into your life?
5. What do you want to leave behind?
6. What's something you wish others knew about you?
7. Who is someone you admire? What qualities do you love about them?
8. What are you looking forward to this week?
9. Who is someone you envy and why?
10. What distracts you from what's truly important each day?
11. If you decided right now that you had enough money, and that you would always have enough, what would you do with your life?
12. When you picture yourself 10 years from now, what do you want to have achieved and experienced?
13. How do you want to contribute your talents and passions to the world? Who could be touched by you?
14. What role does love play in your life?
15. What does friendship mean to you?
16. How did you prioritize your time today?
17. What does living authentically look like to you, and how can you bring more of that into your daily life?
18. What are the core beliefs that guide your decisions? How do they shape your relationships and goals?
19. In what ways can you practice kindness, both toward yourself and others, more intentionally?
20. How do you define balance in your life, and what actions can you take to bring more of it into your routine?

## Source 2: A Chronic Voice — 365 Mindfulness Prompts (Selected)
*Source: https://achronicvoice.com/365-mindfulness-journaling-prompts-for-self-improvement/*

### January (New Beginnings)
1. What is one big or small goal you'd like to achieve by the end of the year? Can you visualise how you'll feel once you've reached it?
2. What is one thing you can commit to today, so that your near or distant future self will thank you for?
3. What do you hope to reinforce through this journaling practice?
4. What are you excited about right now? It could be an upcoming event, or simply taking some downtime at the end of a long day.
5. What are you able and willing to let go of this week?
6. When do you feel most energised? How can you use that energy for yourself and the greater good?
7. Do you have a dream that is achievable yet unfulfilled? Dream about it a little more today, and write a few actionable steps.
8. What do you value most in life? Are you standing by these values and how so?
9. What would you do if you had an extra hour every day, and why?
10. What was your biggest mistake in life, and what did you learn? How can this lesson help you in future?
11. What will you do today that will benefit you tomorrow?
12. Bad moods happen. When you feel like you're in one of those moods, how can you turn things around?
13. What is your dream destination and who would you like to accompany you on that trip?
14. Describe yourself in 10 words. Do you like what you see? If not, how can you change that?
15. What's your deepest secret desire? What stops you from revealing it to others?
16. What helps you stay focused when you feel your mind wandering off?
17. What is something in your life that you don't do enough of, but think you should?

### February (Loving Kindness)
1. List five things that you love about yourself.
2. What does unconditional love mean to you? Would you like to give and receive more of that?
3. What do you love most about your body — your eyes, how hard it works for you, or even your scars?
4. What is one thing you can do to show love to someone who matters today?
5. What are three things you love about life in general?
6. What is your love language? How do you like to be shown love from others?
7. List 10 acts of kindness that someone has done for you.
8. Are you happy in your current relationship with yourself? What can you do to deepen that bond?
9. Is there someone whom you've lost in your life and miss? Write a letter in memory of them.
10. What is your most cherished possession, and what significance does it hold?
11. Who fully understands and 'gets you' as a person? Why do you think that is?
12. What are you passionate about in life, and how can you follow that passion?
13. What are your pitfalls as a friend or partner? How can you work on improving these?
14. Are there any relationships in your life that could do with improvement? How can you attempt to make things better?
15. Which relationships drain you? Why? What characteristics of those relationships feel unhealthy?

### March (Transformation & Empowerment)
1. What gives you strength, especially when the going gets tough?
2. What was the event where you showed the most courage in your entire life?
3. What negative thought patterns do you want to break, and how can you start doing so?
4. What is something you need to be truthful to yourself about?
5. "Today I believe that I can…"
6. What does having self-confidence mean to you?
7. What inspires you to take positive action?
8. It's important to forgive yourself to move forward with more freedom. What will you forgive yourself for today?
9. What do you envision in your future? Can you imagine a more empowered one?
10. What are your best qualities, and how can you use them to create a better life?
11. What do you want to improve on? It can be a hobby, skill, or personal trait.
12. When did you last break a rule? How did you feel and what were the consequences?
13. Which teacher or mentor had a real impact on your life? Was it good or bad? Why?
14. What is your greatest fear? How can you work towards overcoming it?
15. Think about your most embarrassing experience. Write about it — push through feeling uncomfortable.
16. When you leave this world, what do you want to be most remembered for?
17. Write about a time you stepped out of your comfort zone. What did it bring you?
18. How can you brighten someone else's day? Is this something you can do often?

### April (Imagination & Hope)
1. If you were to write a book about your life, what would the title be?
2. If a genie were to grant you five wishes right now, what would you wish for?
3. What is worrying you the most right now? Are these worries real or unfounded?
4. If money wasn't an issue, what would you do with your time?
5. If you could travel backwards or forwards in time, would you, and what would you change or want to see?
6. Write about a time when everything you'd hoped would happen, did.
7. Think back to your childhood. What was your favourite book, TV show, or video game?
8. What do you miss most about being a kid, if at all?
9. What are your current life goals? How are you working towards them?
10. Write a letter to your child self. What do you think is most important to convey?
11. If your body could send you a clear, direct message, what do you think it would say with the most urgency?

### May (Self-Acceptance)
1. Write down one thing you can see, smell, hear, touch, and taste right here and now.
2. Which five things would you save if you had to leave your home in an emergency?
3. Discuss a time when you listened to your intuition. Was that helpful?
4. What is one small thing that can make your day or totally turn it around?
5. What has been your favourite memory of the year so far? What do you treasure about it?
6. What was the first thought you had this morning? Is it a daily occurrence?
7. What is one small thing you can do every day that will have positive compounding effects on your life?
8. What imperfections do you have? Which do you most value and why?
9. What are your favourite aspects about your personality? How does that set you apart in a good way?

### June (Maintaining Balance)
1. Think about a time in your life that you felt most balanced and aligned with your values. What was happening?
2. What makes you most uncomfortable? Is there something you can do during those moments to feel more at ease?
3. What are some toxic habits that you need to give up? What would motivate you to give them up?
4. What would a better work-life balance look like to you?
5. How can you realistically add more mindfulness into your everyday life?
6. Name a big decision that you've been weighing. How will this decision impact your life?
7. Are you good at communication? What are some ways you can be better at it?
8. How do you cope with being wrong? Do you admit it or argue against it?
9. Close your eyes and take deep breaths for 3 minutes. What thoughts came into your head? Write them down.
10. What have you learned this year so far?
11. What makes you feel most alive? How can you strive towards more of such moments?
12. Do you feel supported in your life? Who belongs to your support system?
13. List 10 things that are within your control that you can use to improve the quality of your life.
14. List 10 things that you can't control yet worry you. Try to let them go — focus on what is in your power.

### November (Gratitude)
1. How have you shown appreciation lately?
2. What do you appreciate most about your friends or family?
3. What positive legacy do you want to leave in this world?
4. What's one change that you think would make the world a better place?
5. Let's try some gratitude: List 10 things you're grateful for in life right now, and why each is important.
6. Write a short letter of gratitude to someone who means a lot to you.
7. What is something you can do to show more love to yourself?
8. List 10 little things in life that make you smile.
9. What was the best day in your life so far?
10. How can you show your appreciation to service staff in your everyday life?
11. What are three things you can be more grateful for in your life, despite circumstances?

## New Additions (100+) — Stoicism, Philosophy & Buddhism
*(Added in session March 2026)*

### Stoicism & Virtue
1. What would a person of perfect self-control do in my current situation?
2. What am I clinging to that is actually outside of my control?
3. If I lost everything I have today, what would still remain that defines me?
4. Am I being harmed by the event itself, or by the story I'm telling myself about it?
5. How can I turn my current obstacle into an opportunity for growth?
6. If today was my last day, would I be proud of how I spent it?
7. What desire is making me a slave right now?
8. How can I better align my actions with reason today?
9. What is the most virtuous response to the person who frustrated me today?
10. Am I pursuing reputation or character?
11. What am I worrying about that won't matter in five years?
12. How can I practice voluntary discomfort today to strengthen my resilience?
13. What does 'living in accordance with nature' look like for me right now?
14. Whose opinion am I chasing, and why does it matter to me?
15. What is one thing I can stop doing to simplify my life?
16. Am I reacting to my impressions or to reality?
17. What would Marcus Aurelius say to me about my current stress?
18. How can I be a steady cliff against the waves of emotion today?
19. What is the difference between my needs and my wants right now?
20. What is the smallest step I can take toward self-mastery?
21. How did I fail to live up to my principles today, and what would I do differently?
22. What 'insignificant' thing am I blowing out of proportion today?
23. How can I find joy in the strictly necessary?
24. If I were to witness my life from a distance, what advice would I give myself?
25. Am I wasting time on things that don't satisfy my soul?
26. What part of my character needs the most honest work right now?
27. What am I taking for granted that I would desperately miss if it were gone?
28. Am I prepared for the worst-case scenario I'm fearing?
29. How can I turn anger into curiosity today?
30. The things 'not in my control' — can I truly release them, just for today?
31. What is a habit of weakness I keep returning to, and what virtue could replace it?

### Philosophy & Life Reflection
1. If I could choose my parents, would I still choose mine? Why?
2. What is a truth about myself that I'm afraid to admit?
3. If I were to write my own eulogy, what would I want it to say?
4. What does 'home' feel like to me — is it a place, a person, or a feeling?
5. If I had to live this same life over and over forever, would I be at peace with it?
6. What is the 'good life' for me, personally, beyond what society defined?
7. Am I the hero of my own story, or a side character in someone else's?
8. What is the most important lesson I've learned from a mistake?
9. How has my definition of 'success' changed since I was 20?
10. If I could change one thing about my past — knowing the trade-offs — would I?
11. What is the most courageous thing I've ever done?
12. What does it mean to be 'authentic' in a world of performance and social media?
13. If I were the last person on Earth, how would I spend my time?
14. What is a 'calling' I've been ignoring or delaying?
15. What is the value of silence in my life?
16. Am I listening to my intuition or my ego right now?
17. What is a part of myself — a 'shadow' — that I haven't integrated yet?
18. What does forgiveness look like for the person I haven't forgiven?
19. What is the relationship between my freedom and my responsibility?
20. If I could ask one question to a person who has passed away, what would it be?
21. Am I seeking comfort or growth right now?
22. What 'weight' am I carrying that isn't mine to carry?
23. What is a belief I held five years ago that now seems limiting?
24. What is the most selfless thing I've done recently?
25. Am I living for my 'resume virtues' or my 'eulogy virtues'?
26. What is the most 'alive' I've felt in the last month?
27. What is a secret I'm keeping from myself?
28. What is the most important conversation I need to have?
29. How do I define 'integrity' in my day-to-day choices?
30. What is the 'noise' in my life that I need to tune out?
31. If there were no limits, what would I be doing with my one wild and precious life?
32. Am I building a life I believe in, or one that I'm expected to want?
33. What is the story I keep telling myself that might not be true?
34. What would I do today if I knew I was entirely loved and entirely free?

### Buddhism & Mindfulness
1. What is arising in my field of awareness right now — thoughts, sensations, emotions?
2. How can I practice 'non-attachment' to my current problem?
3. What does 'interbeing' mean in the context of my closest relationships?
4. Can I find the humanity in the person I'm struggling with right now?
5. What is the 'second arrow' of suffering I'm shooting at myself?
6. How can I bring more 'Right Speech' into my conversations today?
7. What is a 'seed' of joy I can water today?
8. Can I sit with my discomfort without trying to immediately change or fix it?
9. What is the 'impermanence' of my current mood — how will it feel different tomorrow?
10. How can I practice 'beginner's mind' in a task I've done a thousand times?
11. How can I be more compassionate toward my own failures today?
12. What is the quiet 'noble silence' I can find in my busy day?
13. How am I contributing to the suffering of others, even unconsciously?
14. Can I find the beauty in something 'broken' or imperfect today?
15. What is 'enoughness' for me today?
16. How can I serve others without losing my sense of self?
17. What is the 'middle way' between the extremes I'm currently choosing between?
18. Am I 'arriving' in the present moment, or just passing through it?
19. What is the breath trying to tell me right now if I stop and listen?
20. How can I let go of the 'self' that is feeling criticized or diminished?
21. What suffering is behind the anger or behavior of the people around me?
22. Can I find gratitude in a moment of boredom today?
23. How can I walk through my world more gently, as if I belong to it?
24. Can I let go of the need to be 'right' in a current disagreement?
25. What is the peace that exists beneath all my thoughts if I only look?
26. How can I practice 'equanimity' in the face of today's praise or blame?
27. What is the most compassionate thing I can do for myself tonight before sleep?
28. What would it feel like to be completely at home in the present moment?
